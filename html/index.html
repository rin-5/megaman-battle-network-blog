About Rockman.EXE/Mega Man NT Warrior

Mega Man NT Warrior (Or Rockman.EXE in Japan) is an action anime that aired from March 2002–September 2006 with 5 seasons, of which 2 had been localized, and a movie. The story is the following: in the year 200X, humanity entered a new age— the Network Age —due to the advancement of the internet. Each person carries their Personal Terminal, PET for short, and they contain artificial intelligence programs called NetNavi(or NetNavigator, also referred to as Navi for short), with the owners being called Operator. And though their invention has brought great benefits, hacking, virus spreading and other high-tech crimes are becoming a major problem. Lan and MegaMan.EXE share a 'special' bond, and with it and their friends, they save the world and the Net from several menaces that threaten to destroy them, even going as far as to travel to parallel worlds.

Main Characters

Lan Hikari/Netto Hikari

A 11/12 year old boy who loves NetBattling. He's outgoing, headstrong, and has a strong sense of justice, though he is often lazy, often sleeping in causing him being late to prior engagements (even if he is the one who set them up), and is a poor student in school, much to everyone's frustration and despite MegaMan's nagging, though he has the highest grades in Virus Busting. Lan enjoys sports such as soccer and inline skating, and has a large appetite,joining whenever food is around. As an operator, he is able to concentrate in a way he cannot bring himself to do in other times, and is quick to jump into action to save the day while others panic, even at his own risk. He has a keen sense of deduction even if he has a simplistic view.

MegaMan.EXE /
Rockman.EXE

A NetNavi with a peculiar origin. As an opposite to his operator, MegaMan is polite and well-behaved. He is often reminding Lan of his obligations such as his schoolwork and his laziness, which mostly is the main reason they argue a lot. Despite being more powerful than the opponents he fights, he continues to keep his polite composure. MegaMan is also very loyal to his friends, prioritizing their safety over his. He's shown over and over again to burden the pain over seeing his friends get hurt. These cases resulted in many near death instances.

Seasons

The anime is divided into five seasons: Exe, Axess, Stream, Beast and Beast+.
There is also a movie, called 光と闇のプログラム(Hikari to yami no puroguramu, lit. "Program of Light and Darkness"), which happens between the episodes 30-31 of Stream and adapts events from its respective game (BN5).

Battle Chip Selection

  • Cannon

    Transforms the arm into a cannon.

  • Sword

    Transforms the arm into a sword that disappears when broken.

  • Minibomb

    Transforms the arm into a sword that disappears if broken.

  • Step Sword

    Lunges forwards, then disappears and reappears behind the target, unleashing a cross slash.

  • Shock Wave

    Makes a shockwave go forwards.

  • Area Steal

    Teleports the Navi somewhere they choose.

  • Variable Sword

    A rare, stronger sword that can change its range and do more than its base counterpart.

  • Yo-Yo

    Transforms the arm into a cannon that can shoot a Yoyo.

  • Synchro Chip

    Anime exclusive: If the bond between Navi and Operator is strong and both are inside a Dimensional Area allows the Navi and Operator to become one in the real world.
